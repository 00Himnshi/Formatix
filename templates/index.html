Formatix: a smart solution to automate the entire Research Paper formatting process.


In One Click transform your plain text into a perfectly formatted IEEE-compliant document.

What Formatix Offers?

TXT to DOCX Conversion

Accepts a plain text (.txt) input file and generates a formatted IEEE-compliant document in .docx format, ready for download.

Automatic Title Page

Generates a professional title page with the title, authors, affiliations, and abstract.

Heading Recognition & Formatting

Identifies main sections, subsections, and sub-subsections in the text and applies IEEE-specific formatting styles.

Body Text Consistency

Ensures proper font size, line spacing, and paragraph formatting throughout the document.

Margin Compliance

Automatically sets 1-inch margins on all sides, adhering to IEEE guidelines.

General Formatting Rules

The uploaded text file must begin with the Abstract section.
Do not include the title of the paper or author names, as these will be automatically formatted.
Separate each level section (e.g., Abstract, Keywords, Introduction, Methodology, References) with a blank line before each heading.
Do not leave blank lines between the content within a section.
